Journey
My Journey

Early Interests and Academic Background

From a young age, I was fascinated by the natural world, which led me to pursue a pre-degree in Biology Science. This foundation taught me the importance of observation, detail, and systematic study.

Delving into Mathematics

With a strong analytical mindset, I continued my education with a degree in Mathematics. Here, I developed a deep appreciation for logic, problem-solving, and the beauty of patterns and structures. These skills have been invaluable in my approach to design and coding.

Discovering the World of Coding

Recognizing the power of technology to transform ideas into reality, I enrolled in one of the best coding institutes in Kerala. My training covered a wide range of programming languages and development frameworks, laying the groundwork for my future career in web design.

Transition to Web Design

Equipped with a diverse educational background and technical skills, I found my true passion in web design. The field allowed me to combine my scientific precision, mathematical logic, and coding expertise with creativity. I began developing websites that are not only functional and user-friendly but also visually engaging.
